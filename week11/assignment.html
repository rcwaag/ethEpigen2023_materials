This enrichment analysis tests, whether those genes are are located within differentially methylated regions (DMRs) show an enrichment for any biological processes compared to ALL genes that are located on the same chromosome. The most striking process seems to be “positive regulation of cell adhesion” which shows a particularly strong fold change, meaning that genes within DMRs seem to be especially involved in this process. Signaling in general seems to be an important factor, as several processes are enriched (regulation of signaling, Cell communication, cell adhesion, signal transduction).
